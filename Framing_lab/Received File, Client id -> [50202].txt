It’s not easy being a soldier sent out to war to achieve impossible victories. 
That’s why you need the right motivator and words of encouragement to get your spirits up. 
Tanya Degurechaff may not be the most wholesome leader, but she definitely knows how to capture her battalion’s attention. 
With smell of all out war looming over the Empire, Tanya and her battalion are assigned to fight the Free Republic in Africa. Of course, 
the soldiers are nervous and scared about how the fight will turn out so before going into battle, 
Tanya gives a rousing speech before dismissing her troops.
